// FAQ

What counts as an API request? +
Each call to our AI services (COGI, Guardian, Docufy, etc.) counts as one API request. Failed requests don't count against your limit.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Google Pay, and mobile money (M-Pesa) for Kenya-based customers.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? +
Our Free tier gives you 100 API calls monthly forever. For paid plans, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee.
What happens if I exceed my limit? +
We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, requests will be queued or you can upgrade instantly.
