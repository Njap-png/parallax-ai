Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow certain features to function properly.

How We Use Cookies

BridgeMind uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly, including authentication and security features.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings like theme preference (dark/light mode).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our platform to improve it.

Cookie Types We Use

  • auth_token: Keeps you logged in (30 days)
  • theme: Remembers your theme preference (1 year)
  • cookies: Tracks your cookie consent (1 year)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Fonts: For font delivery
  • Groq API: For AI services
  • Paystack: For payment processing

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
